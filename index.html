About Me

Driving innovation at the intersection of geospatial technology, environmental science, and artificial intelligence

I'm a Geospatial Data Scientist with over 6 years of experience transforming complex environmental data into actionable insights. My expertise spans remote sensing, hydrological modeling, machine learning, and cloud infrastructure, with a focus on sustainable water resource management and climate adaptation.

Currently based in Lyon, France with a Swiss B work permit, I've led technical implementations across Europe and the Middle East, delivering national-scale geospatial solutions for government agencies, research institutions, and private sector clients. My work bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and practical applications that drive real-world impact.

I'm passionate about developing uncertainty-aware methods for water and land management in arid environments, particularly in Saudi Arabia where I see immense potential for AI-driven environmental solutions.
